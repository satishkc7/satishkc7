## About

I build intelligent systems that move AI from proof-of-concept to production - turning complex data problems into operational systems that deliver measurable business outcomes.

Currently consulting at **Bridgital**, where I architect distributed automation systems, deploy generative AI agents with compliance guardrails, and build semantic search pipelines on AWS Bedrock. Previously built OCR and document classification systems at **Bitpoint** and delivered a multi-domain legal RAG system at **Hartsfield & Associates**.

**MS in Data Science** (4.0 GPA) with a **BE in Mechanical Engineering** and a peer-reviewed publication on queuing theory.
